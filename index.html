Major projects

Crossfit Novi

Official console website

Crossfit Novi is a set of tools developed to let admins easily manage the Crossfit Novi gym:
  • A complex website to where it's possible to manage users, add lessons, send notifications and more (available for admins only)
  • An iOS and Android app where athletes can subscribe to lessons, receive notifications and more

MyTrains

Visit website

MyTrains lets you check Trenitalia timetables and save your favorite routes to easily verify trains status:
  • All trains timetables
  • Trains delay
  • Italian stations realtime timetables
  • Official comunications from Trenitalia

OpenThem

Download on Chrome Web Store

OpenThem is a Chrome extension which lets you open your favorite websites when launching Google Chrome, with some cool options:
  • Open websites in incognito mode
  • Automatic synchronizations between all your Google Chromes
  • Open links only on some devices
  • ...and so on

MyAuto

Official website - Google Play Store

With MyAuto you can manage all your vehicles:
  • Get vehicles details automatically (only for italian plates)
  • Track all your expenses
  • Log every maintenance on your car
  • Exports your data to csv files
  • ....and a lot more
